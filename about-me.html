Noah Tesema

Hello my name is Noah Tesema. Im 27 years old and I live in Vancouver, Washington. My parents immigrated to the United States from Ethiopia in the early 1980s at a turbulent time in the countries history. Growing up my sister and I were exposed to all ascpects of Ethiopian culture from the food, to the language and many other customs and traditions. Because of this, being Ethiopian has become an important part of my identity. As a kid I spent most of my time playing competitive chess and soccer. I also deeply enjoyed music.
